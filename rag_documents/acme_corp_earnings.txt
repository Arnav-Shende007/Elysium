COMPANY OVERVIEW
-----------------
Acme Corp (Ticker: ACME) is a diversified industrial conglomerate with operations across aerospace components, industrial automation, and specialty chemicals. Market cap: $48.2 billion. Headquartered in Chicago, IL, with manufacturing facilities in 14 countries.

Q4 2024 FINANCIAL HIGHLIGHTS
------------------------------
Revenue: $12.8 billion (up 6.2% YoY, beating consensus of $12.4B)
  - Aerospace segment: $5.1B (+11.3% YoY) — driven by commercial aviation recovery and defense contract wins
  - Industrial Automation: $4.6B (+3.8% YoY) — moderate growth; China slowdown offset by reshoring demand in North America
  - Specialty Chemicals: $3.1B (-1.2% YoY) — margin pressure from elevated ethylene feedstock costs

Gross Margin: 34.2% (vs. 33.8% prior year) — mix shift toward higher-margin aerospace
Operating Income: $2.14 billion (up 8.1% YoY)
EBITDA: $2.67 billion; EBITDA margin 20.9% (expansion of 40bps YoY)
EPS: $4.82 (beating consensus of $4.55 by $0.27)
Free Cash Flow: $1.89 billion (FCF conversion ratio: 71%)

SEGMENT ANALYSIS
-----------------
Aerospace (40% of revenue):
  - Backlog reached $22.1 billion, up 14% from prior year
  - Won $3.2 billion multi-year contract for next-generation turbine components
  - Risk: Supply chain constraints in titanium sourcing; lead times extended to 42 weeks

Industrial Automation (36% of revenue):
  - North America orders up 9%; Asia-Pacific orders down 4%
  - Launched AI-driven predictive maintenance platform; early adoption positive
  - Risk: Inventory build at distributors; potential destocking in H1 2025

Specialty Chemicals (24% of revenue):
  - Restructuring program announced: closing 2 underperforming plants in Europe
  - Expected $180M in annual savings by H2 2025, with $250M one-time restructuring charges
  - Risk: Regulatory pressure on PFAS-related products; potential remediation liabilities
